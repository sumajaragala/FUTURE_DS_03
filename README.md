# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents a Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard developed in Power BI using the Bank Marketing Dataset. The dashboard provides insights into customer conversion behavior, campaign effectiveness, and funnel performance, helping businesses identify opportunities to improve marketing outcomes and optimize customer acquisition strategies.

## Objectives

* Analyze overall conversion and drop-off rates.
* Identify high-performing customer segments.
* Evaluate conversion performance across contact methods.
* Understand conversion trends across different age groups.
* Monitor key marketing KPIs for data-driven decision-making.

## Key Metrics

* Total Prospects
* Total Conversions
* Conversion Rate
* Drop-Off Rate

## Dashboard Features

* Marketing Funnel Performance Analysis
* Conversion Analysis by Contact Method
* Customer Segment Performance by Job Role
* Age Group Conversion Analysis
* Interactive Filters for Job Role, Contact Type, Age Group, and Campaign Month

## Key Insights

* Cellular contact method generated the highest number of conversions.
* The 25–34 age group contributed the most conversions.
* Management and technician customer segments showed strong conversion performance.
* The overall conversion rate was 47.38%.
* More than half of the prospects did not convert, highlighting opportunities for campaign optimization.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

## Outcome

This dashboard enables stakeholders to track marketing performance, identify conversion opportunities, understand customer behavior, and make informed business decisions to improve campaign effectiveness and conversion rates.
